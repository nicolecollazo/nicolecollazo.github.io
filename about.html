About Nicole

Growing up in Puerto Rico, my mom would always take me to museums and started me out in different art classes like painting, music, and dance. I stuck primarily to ballet but I always found in my free time a passion for reading, which would then inspire me to write. Even when I was a kid and didn’t know how to hold a pen, I would narrate stories to my mom and make her write them. In high school, I was oriented more to math and sciences yet I found a way to implement these artistic passions of mine in everything I did. Attending a liberal arts college in New York City, Eugene Lang College at The New School, I am currently in my third year in the Journalism + Design department. Here I have developed skills such as:

  • Research
  • Interviewing
  • Developing digital and print content
  • Organization within short and strict deadlines
  • among many other things.

In my time as a student, I have written for the university’s newspaper as well as other publications. Take a look at my published works here.
